01 / About

A data engineer with one foot in research and one in operations.

I like problems that sit between technical depth and a real business answer. The best work happens when both have to be right.

Right now I'm a Cloud FinOps & Financial Analysis Intern at SitusAMC, running cost analysis and building financial dashboards across AWS and Azure. The work is about pulling signal out of noisy spend data and making it land with the people deciding what to cut, scale, or shift.

Before that I spent eight months on a research collaboration between USF and Florida Blue, fine-tuning Med42-8B for lung cancer clinical decision support — synthetic dataset generation, QLoRA training, clinical validity checks, and a Hex reporting layer the research team could actually use. We took training loss from 2.20 to 0.10 and beat the baseline by +0.45 ROUGE.

On the side, I spent two years tutoring data analytics and statistics through Knack — 20+ students, consistent 5-star reviews, and a habit of turning intimidating concepts into something my students could actually do.
